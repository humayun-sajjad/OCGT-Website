Senior recommendation

For OCGT specifically, my one-line answer:

Ink Black `#0E1014` for the safest, most timeless, "every premium site uses this" pick — your teal accent then carries the brand identity unchallenged. Or, if you want OCGT to actually own a color in the geotechnik space, Construction Amber `#E8930A` — complementary to your teal, signals "Bau" instantly, and no Berlin geotechnik competitor uses it.
