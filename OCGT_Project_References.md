## Marquee Projects (lead with these on `/referenzen`)

These are the projects to feature prominently — high public recognition, prestige clients, technical complexity, or combinations of all three.

### 1. Elbtower, Hamburg (2019)
Two tiefste Bohrpfähle Deutschlands. Client chain: SIGNA → Bauer Spezialtiefbau → GuD Consult → OCGT team (Bauüberwachung der Probepfähle + 3D-Grundwasserströmungsmodell, Feflow). **Use case:** flagship hero reference. National record, nationally-recognised developer.

### 2. Neue Nationalgalerie, Berlin (2016–2018)
Bauüberwachung Baugrube und Gründungsmaßnahmen. **Use case:** cultural landmark, design by Mies van der Rohe, refurbishment led by David Chipperfield. Photos are dramatic.

### 3. Deutsche Staatsoper, Berlin (2012–2014)
Bauüberwachung Baugrube und Gründungsmaßnahmen + DSV-Unterfangung + Trog-Baugrube. **Use case:** cultural landmark, complex Spezialtiefbau (Düsenstrahlsohle in difficult subsoil), multi-year mandate.

### 4. Museum Barberini, Potsdam (2014–2020)
Six-year involvement: Bauüberwachung Baugrube (2014), Gutachten zu Spundwandbohlen (2015), Hydrogeologische Feldkampagne (2015–2020). **Use case:** long-term client relationship, Hasso Plattner's flagship museum, cross-disciplinary scope.

### 5. Humboldthafen H3-H4, Berlin (2016–2017)
Bauüberwachung + Gutachterliche Stellungnahme zu Pumpversuchen und wasserrechtlicher Erlaubnis. **Use case:** demonstrates the rare A3 + A1 combination (Grundwasserexpertise + Bauüberwachung) which is central to the 2026 positioning.

### 6. Europacity Berlin (Quartier Heidestraße) — multiple buildings (2019–2022)
TRACK (2019), CROWN1 (2021–2022), CROWN2 (2020). Three separate Bauüberwachung mandates in Berlin's most prominent new urban district. **Use case:** shows repeat-client capability and Berlin Großbaustellen specialisation.

### 7. Bundesministerien, Berlin (2017)
BMEL (Bundesministerium für Ernährung und Landwirtschaft) + BMAS (Bundesministerium für Arbeit und Soziales). Bauüberwachung Baugrube und Gründungsmaßnahmen. **Use case:** federal public-sector references — a huge credibility signal for Präqualifizierung and future public tenders. Keep these visible.

### 8. Heizkraftwerk Lichterfelde, Berlin (2014)
Bauüberwachung Gründungsmaßnahmen. **Use case:** energy infrastructure / Vattenfall-tier client, demonstrates industrial-scale capability.

### 9. DIBT-Forschungsvorhaben "Modellierungen zu Injektionssohlen" (2015–2016)
Grundwassermodellierung 3D-Strömungs- und Transportmodell, Feflow. Research contract from the Deutsches Institut für Bautechnik. **Use case:** signals scientific/technical authority — you have done research that sets the standard others follow.

### 10. Tegelquartier (Nord + Süd), Berlin (2017–2018)
Multiple Bauüberwachungs-Mandate. **Use case:** directly adjacent to the new Schumacher Quartier / Tegel development (the 46-hectare timber-construction district starting Hochbau 2026) — positions you as the obvious local expert with site-specific Baugrund knowledge.

### 11. DGEN 1200 MW Power Project, Bharuch, Gujarat, India (2011)
Bauüberwachung Baugrube und Gründungsmaßnahmen. **Use case:** international capability, industrial mega-project scale.

### 12. Berlin Pro, Helmholtzzentrum (2013)
Hydrogeologisches Gutachten für Teilchenbeschleuniger-Bauwerk. **Use case:** unusual / scientifically prestigious. One-liner sells itself.

### Grundwassermodellierung (13 projects)
Strong specialised portfolio across Feflow (the dominant tool), MODFLOW, and PCGeoFim. Projects range from Berlin S-Bahn (S21) to Qatar desalination contexts to industrial Altlasten. Use this category to anchor the **A3 — Betriebsbeauftragter Grundwasser** and **A4 — Geotechnische Beratung** pages.

### Hydrogeologische Gutachten & Feldkampagnen (4+ projects, often multi-year)
Museum Barberini (5-year field campaign), Blockdammweg (3-year), Helmholtzzentrum (research-grade). Use for the **A4 — Geotechnische Beratung** page to demonstrate advisory / expert-witness credibility.

### Vergabe & Ausschreibungen (embedded in 2 mandates)
Rosenthaler Straße and Tegelquartier included Vergabe alongside Bauüberwachung. This service is underrepresented in the portfolio as a standalone offering — consistent with the current site positioning it as a value-add on top of A1 rather than a primary entry point. The new **A2** page should probably cite these two as representative references and not try to look more voluminous than reality.

## Where This Data Is Missing / Needs Clarification

Before the migration chunk runs, you should confirm or fill these gaps:

**1. Client names per project.** The live site only shows project names and locations, not clients. For the strongest Referenzen page, each entry should name the client (e.g. "Client: GuD Consult GmbH, im Auftrag der SIGNA Gruppe, Hauptauftragnehmer: Bauer Spezialtiefbau GmbH"). Some you already know (Elbtower, BMEL, BMAS, Staatsoper). Others you will need to look up in your own records. If any are under NDA, mark those as "Kunde auf Anfrage."

**2. Reality Capture / Drone projects.** The entire list above is Geotechnik work — there are **no drone / photogrammetry projects listed**. This is significant: your new site's B1–B3 pages will have no historical references to show. You have two options:
- (a) Add 3–5 drone projects from 2023–2025 to this list before launch, even if they are smaller-scale test cases
- (b) Frame B1–B3 as "Neue Leistung seit 2023/2024" and use 3DGS demos / synthetic references instead of client references until real ones accumulate

Option (a) is stronger commercially. You presumably have *some* drone work on record — even one or two site-documentation jobs would be enough for v1.

**3. Post-2023 projects.** The live list stops at 2023. Anything you have worked on in 2024 and 2025 should be added before the new site launches — a reference page that trails two years behind current date signals a dormant firm.

**4. Project outcomes / metrics.** None of the entries include outcomes ("reduced Nachtragsforderungen by €X", "detected deviation in week Y saving €Z"). The strategy doc flagged this as a key missing piece. For the top 5–10 marquee projects, write one sentence of commercial outcome where you can. For the rest, list-format is fine.

**5. Photos.** The live site uses cm4all-hosted images. These must be downloaded and re-hosted in the new `/static/images/refs/` folder — the cm4all URLs will stop working once DNS cuts over.
